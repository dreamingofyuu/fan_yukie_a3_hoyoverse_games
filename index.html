Genshin Impact

Platform Played on: PC/Mobile

Number of Months played: 6

Money spent: $0

Favourite Character: Xiao

Favourite Event: Lantern Rite

Personal Rating: 9.5/10

Reason: I've been playing Genshin since the release and I've had my fair share of ups and downs. There have been times in which I've stopped playing for months on end. I was first attracted to this game by its beautiful graphics despite it being free to download and play. The storylines, animations, character development, and gameyplay is what allows me to give Genshin such a high rating. This game was my comfort game throughout the years in which COVID had locked me in my room. However much I love the game, I took 0.5 off my rating due to its tedious committment system. As a free-to-play player, I cannot afford to spend copious amounts of money to abtain the characters I want. Thus I need to grind out the game and work for the in-game currency. With academics taking much of my time, I simply could not commit to the game anymore. Nonetheless, I will occasionally get back on the game just to see my favourite characters and participate in some limited time events when I get the chance.
